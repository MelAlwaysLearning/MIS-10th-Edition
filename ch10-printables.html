Chapter 10 Bingo — Call List & Instructions

ISM3011 | Read the CLUE aloud — students must identify the term on their card

How to Play

  1. Distribute one Bingo card (pages 4–6) to each student. Cards 1–4 have different layouts.
  2. Read the clue aloud. Do NOT read the term in parentheses.
  3. Students mark the matching term on their card using a pen, marker, or coin.
  4. First student to get 5-in-a-row calls "BINGO!" Verify and award a prize.

★ Starred terms are quiz answers — they appear on EVERY card.

Call List
ERP ★ — "Enterprise Resource Planning — integrates ALL business functions into one unified system with a shared database"
TPS ★ — "Handles routine, day-to-day business transactions — sales, payroll, and orders processed at high speed"
MIS ★ — "Provides reports and summaries to support managerial decisions — takes TPS data and aggregates it"
DSS ★ — "Provides models and data analysis tools to help tackle complex, semi-structured decisions"
EIS ★ — "Designed for senior executives to monitor key performance indicators and strategic performance"
KPI — "Key Performance Indicator — a measurable value showing how well an organization achieves its objectives"
Business Intelligence — "Technologies and strategies for analyzing business data to support decision-making — Tableau, Power BI"
Data Warehouse — "A central repository of integrated data from all IS sources — optimized for analysis, not transactions"
Dashboard — "A visual display of key metrics and KPIs — the main output of an EIS for executive use"
Functional IS — "An information system serving a specific business department — accounting IS, HR IS, marketing IS"
Silo — "When information is trapped within one department and doesn't flow to others — the problem ERP solves"
Integration — "Connecting separate IS so data flows seamlessly between departments — the core value of ERP"
Real-Time Processing — "Each transaction is processed immediately as it occurs — ATM withdrawals update your balance instantly"
Batch Processing — "Transactions are collected and processed in groups at a scheduled time — payroll runs every two weeks"
OLAP — "Online Analytical Processing — enables multi-dimensional analysis and drill-down capability in BI tools"
SCM — "Supply Chain Management — coordinates the flow of goods, information, and finances from suppliers to customers"
CRM — "Customer Relationship Management — manages all customer interactions to build loyalty and maximize value"
HRM — "Human Resource Management IS — handles employee lifecycle from recruitment to payroll to performance"
Accounting IS — "Tracks financial transactions, manages accounts payable/receivable, produces financial statements"
Feedback Loop — "When IS output becomes input for another IS — TPS data feeds MIS, MIS alerts feed DSS decisions"
Decision Support — "IS that helps managers analyze complex problems — not automatic decisions, but tools to inform judgment"
Operational IS — "IS supporting day-to-day operations at the lowest organizational level — includes TPS"
Strategic IS — "IS that provides competitive advantage by supporting long-term strategic decisions — includes EIS"
Enterprise System — "A large-scale IS spanning the entire organization — ERP is the prime example"
