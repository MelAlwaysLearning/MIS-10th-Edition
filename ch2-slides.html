Porter's Five Forces — Every Industry Has These Threats

⚔️
Rivalry
Coke vs Pepsi. McDonald's vs Burger King. Intense competition among existing players in the industry.
🚪
New Entrants
When Uber entered the taxi market, they disrupted the whole industry. New entrants threaten established players.

Substitutes
Zoom didn't just compete with Skype — it substituted for the entire business travel industry. Different product, same need met.

Amazon's marketplace gives buyers so many choices that sellers have almost zero pricing power. Buyers are king.

Supplier Power
TSMC makes 90% of the world's advanced chips. Apple, NVIDIA, AMD all depend on them. That's massive supplier power.

📚 Key Distinction

BPR vs. Incremental Improvement — Don't Mix These Up

🔧 Incremental Improvement

  • Small, gradual tweaks to existing processes
  • Working within current structure
  • Example: Blockbuster adding a late-fee waiver
  • Example: adding one more checkout lane
  • Result: marginal improvement

💥 BPR (Business Process Reengineering)

  • Fundamental rethinking from scratch
  • Throw out the old process entirely
  • Example: Netflix inventing streaming (not improving VHS)
  • Example: Amazon replacing stores with algorithms
  • Result: dramatic transformation
⚡ Exam trap: BPR is NOT "improving" a process. It is redesigning it from zero. If the question says "minor tweaks" or "gradual improvement" — that is NOT BPR.

📚 Core Concept

Strategic IS — When Technology Becomes Your Moat

A Strategic IS provides a company with a sustainable competitive advantage that rivals cannot easily copy.
🛒 Amazon's Recommendation Engine — analyzes billions of data points to suggest what you'll buy next.
→ Drives 35% of Amazon's total revenue. No competitor has matched it.
🏪 Walmart's RFID Supply Chain IS — tracks every single product from factory to shelf in real time.
→ Cut inventory costs by billions. Competitors spent years trying to replicate it.
🚗 UPS's ORION Routing IS — AI optimizes 55,000 drivers' routes in real time, avoiding left turns.
→ Saves $400M/year and 10 million gallons of fuel. Pure IS competitive advantage.
🎵 Spotify's Discover Weekly IS — machine learning creates a personalized playlist for 400M users every Monday.
→ Became the #1 reason people stay subscribed. Pure IS advantage over Apple Music.

📚 Core Concept

Three Ways IT Creates Competitive Strategy

Click each strategy to reveal company examples ↓

💰 Cost Leadership
Use IS to become the lowest-cost producer in the industry. Win on price.
Walmart's supply chain IS. Amazon's automated warehouses. Spirit Airlines' lean IT operations.
✨ Differentiation
Use IS to offer something unique that no one else can. Win on distinctiveness.
Netflix's recommendation engine. Apple's seamless device ecosystem. Starbucks' loyalty app predicting your order.
🎯 Focus / Niche
Use IS to serve a specific market segment better than anyone else. Win in your lane.
Peloton serving premium fitness. Etsy serving handmade sellers. LinkedIn serving professional networking only.
